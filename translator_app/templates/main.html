What is WEB3?

This vision sees a fully decentralized web that gives control back to users, moving away from today's internet dominated by centralized entities like corporations or governments. This new web is built on a decentralized network infrastructure known as blockchain. The most common example of this is Bitcoin, a blockchain-based system. Other projects, such as Ethereum, have expanded and refined blockchain's principles and introduced the concept of smart contracts.

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating machines and software that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning and adapting to new situations, understanding and interpreting language, recognizing patterns, problem-solving, and making decisions. Today the use of artificial intelligence is becoming an everyday occurrence for us and this is just the beginning of the big story.

Anonymity?

Blockchain technology, embedded in the Web3 ethos, shares a key trait with AI: autonomy. A powerful tool for privacy, blockchain ensures secure transactions and information sharing, thanks to its inherent decentralization and immutability. Coupled with AI, it becomes even more powerful, empowering users to retain control over their data while benefiting from AI's analytical prowess.
